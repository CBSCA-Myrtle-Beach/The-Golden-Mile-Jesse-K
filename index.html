The Golden Mile

Myrtle Beach's Most
Coveted Coastline

Stretching along North Ocean Boulevard between 31st and 52nd Avenue North, The Golden Mile is the only oceanfront stretch in Myrtle Beach free of high-rise hotels — a mile of private residences, historic estates, and exclusive communities where the beach belongs to those who live here.

Myrtle Heights

National Register Historic District

Myrtle Heights

Established in 1933 and added to the National Register of Historic Places in 1998, Myrtle Heights is the historic heart of The Golden Mile. Spanning from 32nd Avenue to 46th Avenue North, this neighborhood is defined by classic two-story frame homes, generous porches, and a distinctive architectural character that has been carefully preserved for nearly a century.

Oceanfront estates here command $1.5M to $4M+, while properties further from the shore offer entry from the $300Ks — a rare range of access points into one of the Grand Strand's most storied addresses.

Ocean Village

Private Gated Oceanfront Community

Ocean Village

A private, gated enclave on Ocean Boulevard, Ocean Village is one of the most exclusive addresses on The Golden Mile. With just 33 cottage-style residences ranging from 1,700 to 4,000 square feet, this community delivers the intimacy and privacy that define true coastal luxury.

Homes feature front and back porches, metal roofs, and thoughtfully designed floorplans. Residents enjoy a Live Oak Park, private swimming pool, sidewalks, gazebo, fishing pond, and access to the Dunes Village Tennis Courts — all steps from the beach.

Ocean Forest

Oceanfront Luxury Villas

Ocean Forest

Perched on the northern stretch of The Golden Mile, Ocean Forest is a beautifully manicured community of three-story villa buildings set within a neighborhood of million-dollar homes. The 20-acre property is just steps from pristine sandy beach, offering a resort-caliber lifestyle in a residential setting.

Well-maintained grounds, two large centrally located swimming pools with ample sun decks, and the quiet prestige of one of Myrtle Beach's most desirable oceanfront addresses make Ocean Forest an exceptional opportunity for discerning buyers.

Forest Dunes

Premier Oceanfront Resort Residences

Forest Dunes

Forest Dunes commands one of the most coveted oceanfront positions on The Golden Mile — 18 floors of individually appointed residences with unobstructed Atlantic views. Offering one and three-bedroom layouts, each unit features a full kitchen, private balcony, and finishes that reflect the caliber of this address.

Resort-level amenities include indoor and outdoor pools, whirlpools, lazy river, fitness trails along the ocean, and the seasonal Buckets Bar & Grill — all within a peaceful, low-traffic setting on the north end of Myrtle Beach, minutes from Broadway at the Beach and Restaurant Row.
